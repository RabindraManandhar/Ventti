VENTTI CARD GAME

This is a desktop-version card game. It is one of my earliest projects, that I started to practice my basic JavaScript knowledge in the very beginning of my study in IT.

This Ventti card game has a rule book and its rules are:

  1. This game is played between two players - human(player1) and computer(player2)
  2. Player1 gets the first turn to draw the cards from a deck.
  3. Player2 gets his turn only after the player1 completes his turn to draw the cards.
  4. The winner is declared as follows:
    1. The player whose cards' sum exceeds 21 loses the game.
    2. The player whose cards' sum is most close to 21 wins the game.
    3. When player1 starts drawing cards, s/he can pass the turn to player2 at anytime.
    4. When player1 passes the turn to player2, the player2 starts the game and s/he can also stop his turn as will
